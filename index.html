Traffic accidents and road safety in Fredericton

The objective of the project is to determine the road safety in the city of Fredericton.

Accidents data Year: 2017
Places of interest: Schools and Hospitals
Proximity of accidents to places of interest: 50 meters

Project Plan

  1. Pick the study area

  2. Download the Data

  3. Data observation and preparation

  4. Build the model

  5. Data product sharing

Google Map of Fredericton can be used as a reference to the maps used in the project.

Project description

The Fredericton accident data points are filtered for the year 2017. Public places such as schools and hospitals must be safe and away from the busy traffic roads.

The scope of the project is to determine the safety of people in such buildings as per accidents data for 2017. For determination of safety, the buffers around the buildings are created covering 50 meters radius. The number of accidents within buffers decide the extent of safety.
